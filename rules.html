What are the rules of Badminton

There are different rules in badminton depending on what type, like singles and doubles

Badminton is played to 21 points (best of 3 games) using rally scoring, where a point is scored on every serve regardless of who serves. The server must hit the shuttle underhand below the waist, diagonally into the opponent’s service court. A game must be won by a 2-point margin, up to a maximum score of 30–2
